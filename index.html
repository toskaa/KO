        ##About

Kembrya has been an advocate for human rights since she was able to observe and understand. Born October 5th, 1998 she is a hardcore libra enthusiast! She can often be found researching (fake) zodiacs (on Tumblr), watching Netflix, reading a book, touching up on social issues, chilling, or spending time with the ones she love most. She has a small circle of friends and a large crowd of associates, but she loves everyone she's come in contact with because everything happens for a reason.

Interest involve marching band, pep band, and concert band. Theater tech, book club, impact club, SAAD, and Girls Who Code (GWC) has taken up a lot of time out of her life this past year. She also volunteers for show choir crew to help keep the fine arts program running efficiently.
